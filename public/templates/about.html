Treatment can be done in two ways,traditional medicine treatment or ottamooli treatment. Every season we get a fever, cold, allergy, sore throat... All these diseases come suddenly to us. Vayanadan Ottamooli treatment is deeply rooted in Kerala's traditional Ayurvedic and folk medicine practices. It involves using locally available herbs and natural substances to treat a wide range of ailments and health conditions. These treatments have been passed down through generations and are considered a part of the cultural heritage of the region.

It's important to note that Ottamooli treatments are a part of traditional knowledge and might not have been extensively studied or scientifically validated in the same way modern medicine has been. While some Ottamooli remedies may provide relief for certain minor ailments, their efficacy and safety may vary. As with any form of alternative or traditional medicine, it's recommended to consult with a qualified healthcare professional before using Ottamooli treatments, especially for serious or chronic health conditions.
